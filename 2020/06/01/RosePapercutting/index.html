玫瑰 Just Rose

Jun 1, 2020剪纸

探索事物的本质，发现原本的美。
Explore the nature of things when the beauty reveals itself.

收到一束玫瑰，你可曾仔细观察他？摘下每一片花瓣，拨开花蕊，数一数有多少片？
还只是将其插入瓶中，静静枯萎。
符号化的玫瑰让我们逐渐忽视他本身的美，其他很多的事物也是如此…

Have you ever looked into it when receiving a rose?
Have you ever picked off every petal and counted?
Or just leave it in the vase, rose rotting.
Rose is symbolized while we are getting to ignore its inner beauty and so many other things…

关于我 About Me

嘿！你好，我的朋友，这是白桦用来分享的小博客。
首先感谢维护Hexo引擎的开发者们以及inside主题的开发者Elmore Cheng。多亏了他们，才能让我这样一个计算机小白能搭起这个博客。另外要特别感谢Valine的作者，驱动了本站的评论系统。
然后要感谢所有支持我的家人和朋友，你们的支持是我创作的动力，还要感谢那些每天与我擦肩而过的，素不相识的路人，是你们给了我源源不断创作的灵感。

白桦是一个学习者，对IT，对文学艺术有着无比的热忱，喜欢拍片📷、读书📖、折腾电脑💻、时不时有了灵感也会乱写一通。

最开始，是从公众号做起，初衷是想分享白桦的“作品”，后来因为种种原因，白桦决定弃用了这个公众号（感谢那80个关注者），后来他搭建了这个博客，希望能够把这片角落，与人分享。

虽然不能贡献什么代码，但是时不时挑一些平时的摄影加入CC0社区，也是一件值得高兴的事情，几个月来已经有了八千多下载量，很高兴有人喜欢我的“作品”，如果你在本站看到任何喜欢的摄影作品，白桦会很愿意提供商用授权。

Hi! My friend! This is the tiny blog where I share things.
Firstly, I must appreciate developers who maintain Hexo and the developer of theme inside Elmore Cheng. I can not go so far without them. I should give special thanks to the developer of Valine which powers the comment system.

Then I shall thank my families as well as my friends offering their support all the time, which is my motivation to work. Also, I shall thank all the anonymous people I meet cause you are my inspiration to compose.

I am just a learner with great passion for IT,art and literature. Photography📷,reading📖,try new stuff on my 💻 is all I enjoy. When it comes I write things too.

From the very beginning, I start with a wechat account where I share my work, and abandoned for some reason(thank the 80 followers,your support matters.) And now here we are, I hope to make it work sharing the realm of my soul.

I can barely contribute any codes, so I pick some of my photography and make it free through CC0 license. Downloads reached 8k in a few months, which is such a delight. Be free to use them if you like.

This my way to give back.
